Who Am I?

I'm a third-year Software Engineering student at York University with a deep passion for data science and analytics. My journey into data began with the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate on Coursera, where I gained hands-on experience in data cleaning, analysis, and visualization using tools like R, SQL, and spreadsheets. Currently, I'm expanding my skill set through Microsoft's Power BI course, diving into dynamic dashboards, business intelligence, and turning raw data into compelling stories that drive decision-making. I work as a Data Annotator at DataAnnotation, where I contribute to refining machine learning models by ensuring high-quality, labeled data. My goal is to blend my software engineering foundation with data-driven thinking to build tools and insights that make a meaningful impact.
